Mashed Potatoes

Description

Come on who doesn't like mashed potatoes. Come check this out!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Bacon bits
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft
  2. Mash the potatoes like its mud
  3. Put the mashed potatoes into an oven safe ovenware
  4. Sprinkle bacon bits on top
  5. cover the top with slices of cheese
  6. In the oven until done
