Curry

Description

Just a simple japanese curry to warm up your body!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Beef blocks
  • Onions
  • Curry blocks
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. cook onions, carrots, and potatoes to perfection
  2. pan sear the beef
  3. add them all in a pot with water
  4. put curry blocks and wait for the ingredients to finish
